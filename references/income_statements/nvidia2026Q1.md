# NVIDIA Q1 FY26 Income Statement

> Source: App Economy Insights (appeconomyinsights.com) + SEC EDGAR 8-K (filed 2025-05-28)
> Period: Q1 FY2026 (ending Apr 2025)

## Revenue Breakdown

| Segment | Q1 FY26 Revenue | YoY |
|---------|----------------|-----|
| Data Center | $39.1B | +73% |
| Gaming | $3.8B | +42% |
| Professional Visualization | $0.5B | +19% |
| Automotive | $0.6B | +72% |
| **Total Revenue** | **$44.1B** | **+69%** |

> ⚠️ **Note**: The original App Economy Insights infographic showed Gaming ~$2.8B and used non-GAAP estimates for income metrics. Actual results (SEC 8-K): Gaming was a record $3.8B (+42% YoY, +48% QoQ).

## Income Statement (GAAP)

| Metric | Value | Note |
|--------|-------|------|
| Revenue | $44.1B (+69% Y/Y) | |
| Cost of Revenue | $17.4B | Includes ~$4.5B H20 inventory charge |
| Gross Profit | $26.7B (60.5% margin) | Depressed by H20 export control write-down |
| Operating Expenses | $5.1B | |
| Operating Profit | $21.6B (49.1% margin) | |
| Net Profit | $18.8B (42.6% margin) | |

> ⚠️ **H20 Inventory Charge**: In Q1 FY2026, the US imposed export controls on NVIDIA H20 chips to China. NVIDIA recognized a one-time inventory write-down of ~$4.5B, reducing GAAP gross margin from the typical ~74% to 60.5%. Non-GAAP gross margin was ~73%.
